Privacy Policy

Refi·Mort Estimator  ·  R2ICE Studios  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Short version: Refi·Mort Estimator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations happen entirely on your device.

1. Overview

Refi·Mort Estimator ("the App") is a mortgage and refinance calculator developed by R2ICE Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identifiable information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Financial information (loan amounts, income, credit scores)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Cookies or tracking data of any kind

3. How the App Works

All mortgage calculations, amortization schedules, refinance analyses, and payment estimates are performed entirely on your device using the information you enter. No data is sent to any server. No account or login is required. The App functions fully offline.

4. Third-Party Services

The current version of the App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or data collection services. If this changes in a future version, this policy will be updated accordingly and users will be notified.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit any user data, there is no personal data stored on our servers that could be compromised. Any information you enter into the App remains solely on your device and is cleared when you close the App.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the App evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
